# Couples Mod

Couples Mod is a multiplayer Minecraft NeoForge modification designed to enhance the multiplayer experience by adding meaningful relationships, interactions, and shared progression between players. Whether you want to casually date a friend or propose for marriage, this mod adds immersive features and rewarding mechanics to celebrate your bond.

> **Note:** This mod is designed for multiplayer servers.

## 🌟 Features

*   **Dating & Marriage Systems:** Start a relationship with a simple bouquet and eventually propose with an engagement ring.
*   **Interactive Mechanics:** Kiss your partner to earn experience and level up your relationship.
*   **Shared Progression:** As your relationship levels up, unlock unique perks and buffs when near your partner.
*   **Custom UI:** View your relationship status, partner name, level, and shared statistics through a dedicated interface.
*   **Advancements:** Unlock custom advancements as you reach relationship milestones.
*   **Immersive Effects:** Custom heart particles and sound effects when interacting with your partner.

## 💍 Getting Started

### 1. Dating
To start dating someone, you must craft or find a **Bouquet**. The mod supports bouquets made from various vanilla flowers (Tulips, Poppies, Orchids, Roses, etc.). 
*   **Proposing a Date:** Right-click another player while holding a Bouquet.
*   **Accepting:** The targeted player must **Shift + Right-Click** you with an empty hand to accept the proposal.

### 2. Interactions & Leveling
Once dating, you can interact with your partner to grow your bond:
*   **Kissing:** **Shift + Right-Click** your partner with an empty hand. This grants relationship XP, increments your kiss counter, and spawns beautiful custom particles. (Note: XP has a short cooldown to prevent spamming).
*   **Leveling Up:** Accumulating XP will automatically level up your relationship.

### 3. Gifting
You can surprise your partner with gifts directly from your inventory!
*   **How to Gift:** Hold the item you want to give in your Main Hand, open the Relationship Screen (`G`), and click the **"Send Gift"** button. The item will be magically transferred to your partner's inventory.
*   **Daily XP:** The first gift you send each day (every 24 real hours) grants a massive boost of relationship XP based on the rarity and amount of the item!
*   **Gifted Food 💝:** If you gift a food item, it becomes infused with love! When your partner eats "Gifted Food", they receive a significant bonus to their Saturation, making it much better than regular food.

### 4. Marriage
When your relationship reaches **Level 10**, you are ready for the next big step! You *cannot* propose before reaching Level 10.
*   **Proposing Marriage:** Craft an **Engagement Ring** (available in Diamond, Iron, Gold, and Emerald variants). Right-click your partner while holding the ring.
*   **Accepting:** Your partner must **Shift + Right-Click** you with an empty hand to accept. You will both receive matching rings!

## ✨ Perks and Buffs

Being in a relationship isn't just for show; it provides tangible survival benefits:

*   **Sweet Dreams:** If you sleep in a bed near your partner (within 10 blocks) and wake up successfully, both players will receive long-lasting **Saturation** and **Regeneration** buffs for the next 10 minutes.
*   **Power of Love (Marriage Only):** Married couples who stay close to each other (within 32 blocks) will passively receive a powerful aura of buffs, including **Haste II, Regeneration I, Speed I, Health Boost II, and Luck I**.

## ⚙️ Installation

1. Ensure you have [NeoForge 1.21.1](https://neoforged.net/) installed.
2. Download the latest version of Couples Mod.
3. Drop the `.jar` file into your Minecraft `mods` folder.
4. Launch the game and enjoy!
